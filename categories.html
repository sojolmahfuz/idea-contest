Who Can Participate?

In ‘CUB Idea Innovation Contest 2022’, the participants will take part in two categories: Junior Innovator and Associate Innovator.

Junior Innovator

The students from class 8-12 from different schools and colleges will formulate team and participate in the contest.

Senior Innovator

The students from different department and discipline from within the Canadian University of Bangladesh will formulate team and participate in the contest.
