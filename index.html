Transform for Smart Bangladesh 2041

CUB Idea Innovation Contest 2022’ is to drive inclusive efforts to connect the pre-university (schools and colleges) university students having technology talents through motivation, inspiration, encouragement and active engagement in a team to identify a challenge to solve for “Smart Bangladesh 2041”. This would enable and empower the young and enthusiastic technology talents of the country to have the exposure to the best of the breed of innovative and emerging technologies. The department of CSE in association with CUBCS intends to organize “CUB Idea Innovation Contest” on a regular basis in support and in collaboration with the department and other concerns connected to Canadian University of Bangladesh for providing a unique platform and opportunity for grooming up the future technology leaders in Bangladesh and at the same time create mass awareness about the latest technologies.
